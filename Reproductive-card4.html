Reproductive Age: Sexual Health and Well-being

Understand sexual health, wellness, and the importance of maintaining a healthy sexual life during reproductive years.

Sexual Health Overview

Sexual health encompasses emotional, mental, and physical well-being in sexual relationships, which requires respect, consent, and understanding between partners.

Safe Sexual Practices

  • Always use contraception to prevent unwanted pregnancies and STIs.
  • Get regular STI screenings.
  • Communicate openly with your partner about sexual health.
  • Respect each other's boundaries and preferences.

Sexual Wellness Tips

  • Maintain a healthy lifestyle with balanced nutrition and exercise.
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques or hobbies.
  • Address any concerns with a healthcare provider to ensure reproductive and sexual health.
