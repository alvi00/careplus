Reproductive Age: Fertility and Reproductive Health

Understand the key factors that influence fertility and reproductive health during the reproductive years.

Introduction

Fertility and reproductive health are essential aspects of life during the reproductive age. It involves the well-being of the reproductive organs and the ability to conceive and carry a pregnancy.

Factors Affecting Fertility

Factor Effect
Age Fertility decreases with age, especially after 35 years.
Health Conditions Conditions like PCOS, endometriosis, and obesity can affect fertility.
Lifestyle Factors Smoking, alcohol use, and poor nutrition can impair fertility.
Stress High stress levels can disrupt hormonal balance, affecting fertility.

Reproductive Health Tips

  • Maintain a healthy weight.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Stay active with regular exercise.

When to Seek Help

If you have been trying to conceive for over a year without success or are experiencing irregular periods, it may be time to consult a fertility specialist.
