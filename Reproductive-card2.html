Reproductive Age: Family Planning and Contraception

Explore your options for family planning and contraceptive methods to ensure healthy reproductive choices.

Introduction

Family planning is the practice of controlling the number and timing of children in a family through contraceptive methods.

Contraceptive Methods

Method Effectiveness
Condoms 85% - 98% effective in preventing pregnancy and STIs.
Birth Control Pills 91% - 99% effective when taken correctly.
Intrauterine Devices (IUD) 99% effective, long-term, reversible.
Implants 99% effective, lasts for up to 3 years.

Choosing the Right Method

Consult with a healthcare provider to find the contraceptive method that best fits your needs, lifestyle, and health considerations.
