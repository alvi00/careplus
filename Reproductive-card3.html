Reproductive Age: Pregnancy and Childbirth

Important considerations for a healthy pregnancy and safe childbirth experience.

Pregnancy Care

During pregnancy, proper medical care, nutrition, and lifestyle changes are essential for the health of both mother and baby.

Prenatal Health

  • Get regular prenatal check-ups to monitor the health of the baby.
  • Eat a balanced diet with folic acid, calcium, and iron.
  • Exercise regularly, as advised by your doctor.
  • Avoid alcohol, smoking, and excessive caffeine.

Childbirth

Preparing for childbirth involves understanding your options for delivery, from natural childbirth to medical interventions, and ensuring you have a birth plan in place.
